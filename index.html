ABOUT

Alex Hernandez is a singer-songwriter whose music blends elements of indie folk, soul, and contemporary pop. With a voice that carries both power and vulnerability, Alex crafts songs that resonate with emotional depth and melodic sophistication.

Born and raised in Los Angeles, Alex began writing songs at 15 and has since performed at major venues across the country. His debut EP "Whispers in the Dark" garnered critical acclaim, with singles receiving airplay on NPR and college radio stations nationwide.
